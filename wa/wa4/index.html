hey!

Hi, I'm Kate Craig, a Creative Technology and Design student at the University of Colorado Boulder with minors in Computer Science and Planetary Science. I'm passionate about working at the intersection of technology and design, where creative ideas and technical problem-solving come together to build engaging digital experiences. My interest in this field started early when I attended a game design camp at CU Boulder at around ten years old. It was the first time I realized how much I enjoyed both the creative side of design and the logical challenge of programming. Since then, I've continued exploring ways to combine those interests through projects that involve design, development, and digital media. Today, I'm focused on creating work that blends technology, creativity, and storytelling, and I'm excited to continue developing projects and opportunities in creative technology.
